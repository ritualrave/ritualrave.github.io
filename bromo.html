BIOGRAPHY

Bromo is a Geneva-born producer who lives near the French border and has been making music since his teenage years. His journey into electronic music began after his first free party, where he witnessed a live hardware performance that inspired him to craft his own live sets. Starting with guitar in his early teens and moving into electronic production at 16, Bromo has developed a sound that is spontaneous, emotional, and high-energy.

His music reflects life’s emotions and moments, blending elements of acidcore, hard techno, and hybrid styles. Known for his dynamic and emotionally charged live sets, Bromo creates music that connects with the audience on a deep, unfiltered level. He’s performed at iconic venues like Le Zoo in Geneva and Le Petit Salon in Lyon, leaving a lasting impression on crowds with his versatile performances.

Beyond his acidcore roots, Bromo is branching into the hard techno scene while maintaining his acidcore project. A sound engineer by day, music remains his ultimate form of personal expression and connection. Whether on stage or in the studio, Bromo’s goal is to create authentic music that resonates with others and energizes the underground scene.
