DISTEL

Distel’s music showcases a unique fusion of melancholic melodic acidcore and subtle hardcore influences. With a distinct sound characterized by ominous melodies, ethereal 303 lines, dark atmospheres, and pounding kicks, Distel is carving out his own space in the electronic music realm.

Since 2016, Distel has been organizing parties in his hometown of Ghent, bringing together like-minded fans of the acid sound and creating a space for them to connect and dance. He developed a deep appreciation for the power of music to bring people together and create unforgettable experiences.

Drawing on his experiences as a party organizer and his love of the acid sound, Distel began producing his own music, crafting tracks that combine haunting melodies with driving rhythms and an unmistakable sense of emotion.
