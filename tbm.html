BIOGRAPHY

TBM, a dedicated acid and mental tekno producer from Dresden, Germany, has been developing his unique sound since 2016. Drawn to the depth and complexity of these styles, he finds acid and mental tekno to be ideal for expressing a wide range of emotions and creating immersive, structured soundscapes. For TBM, music is about sharing freedom, passion, and energy, aiming to uplift his listeners and foster a sense of inclusivity on the dancefloor. His tracks resonate with audiences looking for powerful, layered compositions that go beyond simple beats, inviting them into a rich sonic experience.

TBM’s releases on labels such as Hangar Limited and Tekno Noise Records showcase his dedication to the genre and his commitment to pushing his music further with each project. Whether at a small party or a large event, he believes it’s the people who make the experience memorable, creating the energy he loves to amplify.
