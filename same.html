BIOGRAPHY

Introducing Same, a talented musician hailing from Lille, France, whose musical journey into the realms of acidcore and tekno began nearly a decade ago with his inaugural rave experience. Influenced by the rich musical heritage of his grandfather's orchestral background, Same's compositions are characterized by their emotive depth and somber allure, drawing inspiration from esteemed artists such as Acidpach, Na-der, and Sevenum-six.

Same meticulously crafts his melancholic soundscapes, each imbued with a distinct message of communal celebration and care. For Same, music serves as a profound means of self-expression, enabling him to articulate his innermost feelings and foster meaningful connections with his audience.
