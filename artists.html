ARTISTS

Step into the dynamic world of Ritual Rave where genres blend seamlessly and boundaries dissolve. Our artists are masters of sonic fusion, combining elements of acidcore, techno, hardcore, and beyond to craft a sound that is truly their own. With each artist bringing their distinct background and perspective to the table, our collective offers a rich tapestry of sonic exploration that promises to captivate audiences worldwide.


Join us on a journey through diverse sonic landscapes, fueled by our artists' relentless passion for experimentation and innovation. From the haunting melodies of Same to the relentlessly pounding beats of Hypnoise Theory, each artist brings a unique vision to our musical tapestry, resulting in a collection of music that is both diverse and compelling.
