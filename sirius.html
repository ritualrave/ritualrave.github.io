BIOGRAPHY

Sirius, a Belgian artist with French nationality, explores the immersive realms of acidcore and tekno through his captivating live sets. Crafting music that resonates with dark, melodically-driven energy, Sirius focuses on haunting melodies and powerful kicks.

His journey into the world of acid and tekno began around 2017, with a deepening fascination taking root in 2022 after attending a party recommended by a friend. Influenced by Bertha, Acidpach, Choko, UtK, and Mr. Gasmask, Sirius creates his sonic landscapes with meticulous attention to detail.

For Sirius, music is a deeply personal reflection of his emotions, serving as a powerful reminder of past experiences and emotions encountered along his journey. With an unwavering commitment to his craft, Sirius finds solace and connection through his music, making each composition a heartfelt expression of his innermost thoughts and feelings.

Music itself is Sirius's true sanctuary, an integral part of his being that he simply cannot live without. As he continues to evolve as an artist, Sirius remains committed to sharing his passion and emotions with the world through his evocative and captivating soundscapes.
